if you're reading this, things have likely gone south OR you broke your promise. either way, I'm pry angry, so I don't particularly care if you read this or not. thanks. enjoy.

public playlists and film discussion lay in the confines of my null space to be realized only when you're not around. I've tried it once, twice, three times before and was met with careless criticism a dismissal of the highest order which you hardly recognize.

my poetry isn't real poetry and my captions don't seem like me and my thoughts aren't my own, but a perversion of something more clever and if this is my projection of your pretension, your presentation of a narcissistic, self-assured piece of contention, well, then I truly don't know what I'm supposed to say.
