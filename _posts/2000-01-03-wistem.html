WiSTEM is a student-led organization at IIT, founded in 2020. Our goal is to create a community of women in STEM fields, as well as provide support and opportunities across many different majors.

WiSTEM comrpises of an executive board, STEM major represenatives, and general body members. In August 2021, I began serving as the Applied Mathematics representative. Since May 2022, I have been the treasurer on the board.

As treasurer, I meet weekly to discuss, plan, and promote organization events. WiSTEM primarily focuses on professional development, community volunteering, and social events. I handle all event purchases, supplies, and budget requests with IIT. Additionally, I handle our sponsorships with Chase Bank and Virtu Financial; these companies collaborate with WiSTEM in hosting information sessions and connect with IIT students on career/internship opportunities.

When I was the AMAT representative, I was the line of commmunication between the organization and other students in AMAT. I promoted and sent out information regarding semester events to professors and peers, as well as help set up and run the events. I also wrote for our newsletter and opportunities spreadsheet, which comprised of scholarships, internships and research opportunities, clubs, and resources at IIT relating to AMAT.

Aside from events, I love speaking to new and prospective students on behalf of WiSTEM.

Our organization has a very active Instagram account, with members often doing an "Instagram Takeover," which I have done twice thus far. I posted my classes of the day, shared school tips, and had a Q&A session about myself and my major.

Additionally, WiSTEM has a very active Instagram account. Members often do an "Instagram Takeover." I have done the takeover twice thus far: once while I was a math rep, and once as the co-treasurer.
