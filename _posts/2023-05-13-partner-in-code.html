--- title: to my partner in code layout: post date: 2023-05-13 01:58 category: poetry description: "when I can't sleep, I write" author: kjros ---

a year has passed, my darling - but that's not right, is it? for you were never quite mine, though your heart beat too fast just for me, and you searched for the stars with me, and you held me in your arms until I fell asleep. and yet, I still write poetry, write unsent letters, play songs on repeat with the hope of keeping you alive, close, ever-so-bright, like the time bled, like those dog-days, the first time I ever wanted forever. how can I enter those same oppressing suffocating months without someone to breathe life back into me? the chalk-streaked fingers, carefully smudging my skin, christening, or tracing my elegant wrists like beautiful code, our bodies curving together in sticky heat. I can't hear lapping waves without tasting strawberries - the sugary fructose keeping sweat at bay - and without feeling sandy grit between our skin, as I swayed on your shoulders, your bony frame the only thing between me and the ground. maybe I loved you, or maybe I had sun-tinted glasses, but your soft r's and non-existent h's echo when I read your inscription for the hundredth time. I blush at our magnetism, which seemed unavoidable, and dream of a life where everything is back the way it was. endlessly waiting for you in my abandoned, glowing room, and endlessly recieiving you.
