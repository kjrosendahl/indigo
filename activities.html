Women in STEM (WiSTEM)

Chicago, IL



WiSTEM is a student-led organization at IIT, founded in 2020. Our goal is to create a community of women in STEM fields, as well as provide support and opportunities across many different majors.

WiSTEM comprises of executive members, major representatives, and general body members. In August 2021, I began serving as the Applied Mathematics representative. Since May 2022, I have been shadowing for the WiSTEM treasurer position on the executive board.

As part of the board, I meet weekly with other members to discuss and plan events, administration, and other tasks to help the organization run smoothly and successfully. I will also be learning how to handle budgets, our industry sponsorships, and purchases for events.

When I was the AMAT representative, I was the line of commmunication between the organization and other students in AMAT. I promoted and sent out information regarding semester events to professors and peers, as well as help set up and run the events. I also contributed to the opportunities spreadsheet, which all representatives did for their respective major. Here, I updated a spreadsheet comprising of scholarships, internships and research opportunities, clubs, and resources at IIT relating to AMAT.

Aside from events, my favorite WiSTEM activities are the newsletter and takeovers!

A few times a semester, board members and representatives write and publish newsletters on the WiSTEM website. Articles discuss future events, featured women in STEM, editorials, and more! For Vol. 3, Issue 2, I wrote for the section "New in STEM". The article, "Molten Mathematics," reviewed the work of mathematicians studying Surtseyan volcanic bombs in 2021, and discussed the use of computer simulations in research across STEM.

Additionally, WiSTEM has a very active Instagram account. Each Thursday, a member does an "Instagram takeover", and posts stories for other students and members to follow. I have done the takeover twice thus far: once while I was a math rep, and once as the co-treasurer. I posted my classes of the day, shared school tips, and answered questions about my major and myself!
