Hi, I'm Kaylee, also known as kjrosendahl (in one cadence). Welcome to my site.

I'm a fourth year, studying for an MS in Computer Science and BS in Applied Mathematics at the Illinois Institute of Technology (IIT) in Chicago, IL.

This started as a small, independent passion project. My advisor recommended I make a website to showcase my skills and work. I doubled down and studied HTML and CSS, clicked through tutorials on Github pages, and stayed up all night perfecting on-hover shadow boxes.

I think he meant something like a Google site. However, as I've remarked to a friend, "people are only as ambitious as is expected of them." I don't intend on merely fulfilling expectations.

So, I've been growing this site, line by line. I hope to keep learning more about front-end development, and implementing new features in a way that truly reflects my interests, personality, and style. After all, a resume can only do so much.

If you're interested in my work on data filtering, satellite simulations, or Chicago lead poisoning, click here. If you want to learn about my involvement in the best (female-led) organization at IIT, read this. And for fun posts and pictures, go ahead and explore my blog and my picture gallery!

Any questions? Reach out; I'd love to get to know you!
