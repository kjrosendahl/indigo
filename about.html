Hi, I'm Kaylee! Welcome to my site!

I graduated from the Illinois Institute of Technology (IIT) in Chicago, IL, in May 2024. I recieved my Master's in Computer Science and my Bachelor's in Applied Mathematics.

This started as a minor project; my advisor recommended I make a website to showcase my skills and work. I doubled down and began studying HTML, clicked through tutorials on Github pages, and stayed up all night tinkering with on-hover shadow boxes.

He definitely meant something like a Google site. However, as I've remarked to a friend, "people are only as ambitious as is expected of them." I don't intend on merely fulfilling expectations.

Having left a life of academia, I hope to get back into writing and designing, reflecting my interests, personality, and personal flair. After all, a resume can only do so much.

If you're interested in my work on data filtering, satellite simulations, or Chicago lead poisoning, click here. For a little reading material, try this. And for fun pictures, explore my gallery!

Aside from professional endeavors, I live for reading, writing, traveling, cooking, collecting art, creating playlists, watching and rating movies, eating A LOT of food, and making wicked birthday cards.

Any questions? You know where to find me.
