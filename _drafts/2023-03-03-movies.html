I'm not the biggest cinephile on the planet - it's hard for me to sit through a movie and not get distracted.

Regardless, there are a few movies I grew to love (or loved immediately). Read more about them here!

1. Clue (1985)

2. Knives Out (2019), Glass Onion (2022)

3. Spider-Man: Into the Spider-verse (2018)

6. (500) Days of Summer (2009)

Genre: drama
